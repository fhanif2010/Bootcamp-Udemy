Angela`s Revipe fot the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the Dough :

  • 3/4 Cup warm
  • 2 1/4 reaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minuites.
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap Leave it some where to rise for 2 hours
